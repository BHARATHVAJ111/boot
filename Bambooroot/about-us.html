About Bambooroot Technologies & Consultancy Private Limited

Bambooroot Technologies was founded by a team of engineering and manufacturing experts with over 20 years of experience, including significant roles at companies like Caterpillar. Based in Chennai, our founders bring deep expertise in digital manufacturing, industrial engineering, and design.

As an end-to-end engineering solutions provider, we offer tailored services that address the full scope of our clients' needs. Our focus on innovation and quality is complemented by our contractual staffing services, ensuring access to skilled professionals for various projects.

Bambooroot Technologies is committed to delivering practical, sustainable solutions and driving excellence in the manufacturing sector.
