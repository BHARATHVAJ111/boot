Alcohol De-addiction Services

Alcohol addiction is a disease that erodes lives, damages relationships, and tears families apart.

In Madurai, countless individuals struggle with this devastating condition. At Iyengaran Faith Care Centre, we understand the unique challenges faced by those battling alcohol dependence.

That’s why we offer a sanctuary of hope, providing the highest quality de-addiction services with both expertise and compassion.

Drug Rehabilitation Services

Whether you require the intensive support of inpatient treatment, the flexibility of outpatient programs, or ongoing recovery monitoring, Iyengaran Faith Care Centre is here to help. We offer specialized programs like 12-Step facilitation and family counseling to mend relationships and provide support from loved ones. Our dedicated team will empower you with the life skills and strategies necessary to build a fulfilling life free from addiction.

Individual Counseling

In the heart of healing and recovery, individual counseling stands as a cornerstone of personal growth and mental well-being. At Iyengaran Faith Care Centre, this form of therapy is tailored to meet the unique needs of each person, providing a safe space for exploration, understanding, and transformation.
