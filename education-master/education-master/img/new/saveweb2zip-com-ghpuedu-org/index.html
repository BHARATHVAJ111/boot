Welcome to Global Human Peace University Virtual Institute

Global Human Peace University an Independent , Non Academic International Online University Incorporated and Registered under the State of Delaware, USA and Globally Recognized.
Certified by, SCK – a Certification Body which is Accredited by International Accreditation Service ( IAS ) and recognized by International Accreditation Forum ( IAF )
Global Human Peace University�  association with People Forum of India Bharat Sevak Shamaj ( Regd. 866/BK IV/2011 )�  an NGO Partner under Planning Commission – Government of India

In the millennium era a rapid growth of the Internet, the Global Human Peace University was established to enhance the skills of educated and uneducated youth around the world, to create multiple jobs, and to bring home the training classes of the world’s foremost professors from various disciplines to ensure that quality education is available to all.

Global Human Peace University’s online platform online.ghpuedu.org is designed to promote informal learning, to transform the form of distance education into a new digital form, and to provide free online education to underprivileged students from around the world.

The university has formulated policies to work with the culture, distance and the increasing number of students across the borders of the countries to enhance the technical knowledge of the working graduates. Global Human Peace University aims to grant global access to quality Online Virtual Courses for students. Our university is committed to spreading beneficial knowledge that is easily affordable and will benefit not only individual students but also empower entire communities. Global Human Peace University aims to collaborate with International Standard online education organizations to offer specialized educational content on our platform.
