Welcome to Research University

Research University an Independent , Non Academic International Online University Incorporated and Registered under the State of Delaware, USA and Globally Recognized.

Certified by, SCK – a Certification Body which is Accredited by International Accreditation Service ( IAS ) and recognized by International Accreditation Forum ( IAF ).

Research University association with People Forum of India Bharat Sevak Shamaj ( Regd. 866/BK IV/2011 ) an NGO Partner under Planning Commission – Government of India.

In the millennium era a rapid growth of the Internet, the Research University was established to enhance the skills of educated and uneducated youth around the world, to create multiple jobs, and to bring home the training classes of the world’s foremost professors from various disciplines to ensure that quality education is available to all.

Research University ’s online platform online.ghpuedu.org is designed to promote informal learning, to transform the form of distance education into a new digital form, and to provide free online education to underprivileged students from around the world.
