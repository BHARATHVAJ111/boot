Our school, established in 1985 as "Sri Venkateswara Kindergarten English School" in Sattur, began with 90 students and 3 teachers. It received Tamil Nadu government approval in 1992 and was renamed "Sri Venkateswara Nursery and Primary School" in 1998.

Due to increasing enrollment, the school moved to a larger building in Periyar Nagar in 2006. In 2014, we gained approval to offer classes VI-VIII, and in 2016, the school was renamed "Sri Venkateswara Matriculation School" with approval to add classes IX and X.

Admission Procedure

Admissions are given to the eligible candidates in classes LKG & I std on the basis of their, performance in the Admission test. Parents who require admissions to their wards in classes Pre KG to IX std may submit the filled in application form to the principal to register for admissions from the third week of March every year. All those who have registered are called to take the admissions test. The registration does not guarantee admission. The Principal reserves the right to admit or reject any application for admission without assigning any reason. Parents who have got admissions to their wards should produce the following:

Transfer Certificate

Copy of the marks card

Conduct Certificate

Community Certificate

Eligibility Certificate and Migration Certificate

Two Passport size Photographs
