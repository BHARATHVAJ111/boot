We are having 18 spacious and well furnished classrooms. Our School Library have One thousand Five Hundred books which includes Reference books, Fictions, Children Books, Novels, General Knowledge and current affairs, etc. We also provide News papers , Journals and periodicals to develop students' reading skill.

Compulsory computer classes are held for all classes right from Std I and to impart practical training to students, we had a computer lab having 10 systems. We had well equipped lab for Science with latest equipments.

The School has two One classrooms to facilitate the students to learn through Audio Visual method. And also the school possesses a wide range of educational CDs. These are being used for teaching purpose with the help of LCD projectors.

The school offers counselling facility through remedial measures to enhance the functional and development potential of the students. Students are encouraged to approach the teachers with their problems anytime they wish. They are also encouraged to use the complaint box to air their grievances. Career guidance is provided to the students by arranging lectures and seminars.

Discipline

The school, always, insists discipline and Academic Excellence. All the teachers work tirelessly and vigorously to maintain Discipline and Academic Excellence at any cost without restoring to any kind of punishment. The school follows the house system. The students are divided into four houses namely Red, Green, Blue and Yellow and School Captain, School Vice Captain and house captains are elected every year.

Our school started in 1985 as "Sri Venkateswara Kindergarten English School" with 90 students and 3 teachers. In 1992, we received government approval and were renamed "Sri Venkateswara Nursery and Primary School" in 1998. With growing enrollment, we moved to a larger building in Periyar Nagar in 2006. By 2016, we had expanded to include classes up to Std X, becoming "Sri Venkateswara Matriculation School."
