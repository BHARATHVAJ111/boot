Rules & Regulations

  1. Children who have completed the age of 3 years are eligible for admission in L.K.G and those who have completed 5 years are eligible for first standard.
  2. The Birth certificate should accompany the application form.
  3. Children coming from other schools will have to produce Transfer Certificate and Birth Certificate.
  4. Students are selected on the basis of an entrance test and interview.
  5. Each student should bring their Assignment Book to the school every day.
  6. Students should come to school in Uniform daily except on Saturdays.
  7. All students should assemble for the school prayer at 09:10 A.M.
  8. Latecomers will not be admitted into the class without the permission of the Principal.
  9. Leave letters should be sent with the signature of the parents.
  10. After one month of continual absence without permission, the absentee’s name will be struck off from the roll.

Our school started in 1985 as "Sri Venkateswara Kindergarten English School" with 90 students and 3 teachers. In 1992, we received government approval and were renamed "Sri Venkateswara Nursery and Primary School" in 1998. With growing enrollment, we moved to a larger building in Periyar Nagar in 2006. By 2016, we had expanded to include classes up to Std X, becoming "Sri Venkateswara Matriculation School."
